ARAB UROLOGIST’S BEYOND BORDERS

Arab Association of Urology (AAU) invites all the urologists around the world who immigrated to different parts of the world in search of better career opportunities. Through this collaboration we intend to reunite all these urologists to bring them together so that they can share their expertise in the different fields of urology that they specialize.

This initiative will enable to us to stay ahead of the curve on the latest developments in urology and to provide up-to-date guidance in the space of urology, investing in our core experts: norms and standards.

The future of healthcare is partially digital, and we are committed to the support the need to maximize the opportunities of digital technologies via our various learning platforms.
