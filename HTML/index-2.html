WELCOME TO ASU

The Arab School of Urology (ASU) was founded in 2017 as an educational platform of the Arab Association of Urology (AAU). ASU delivers intellectual and surgical skills education through multidisciplinary approach including online education, master classes, live surgery events, and hands-on-training.
